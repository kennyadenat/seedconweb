Cookie Policy for the SeedConnect Africa Platform

Introduction

This website, SeedConnect Africa, utilizes cookies, which are small files downloaded to your device to enhance your browsing experience. This Cookie Policy explains what information cookies collect, how we use it, and the reasons for storing them. Additionally, it provides guidance on how you can manage or disable cookies. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain aspects of the site.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are standard tools employed by professional websites to improve user experiences. They store small pieces of data on your computer, allowing us to personalize your interactions with our site. This page provides further details on the information collected and its uses.

For more general information on cookies, please refer to the Wikipedia article on HTTP Cookies.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for a variety of reasons detailed below. Unfortunately, in most cases there are no industry standard options for disabling cookies without completely disabling the functionality and features they add to this site. It is recommended that you leave on all cookies if you are not sure whether you need them or not in case they are used to provide a service that you use.

Disabling Cookies

You can modify your browser settings to prevent the installation of cookies (refer to your browser's Help for instructions). However, it's important to note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality and features of not only this website but also many other sites you visit. Therefore, we recommend keeping cookies enabled for the best user experience.

The Cookies We Set

When you submit data to through a form such as those found on register pages or subscription forms, cookies may be set to remember your user details for future correspondence.

In order to customize your experience on this site, we provide functionality for setting preferences regarding how the site operates during your use. To remember your preferences and provide a seamless experience, we set cookies to store this information for future interactions with the site.

Third-Party Cookies

In special cases, we use cookies provided by trusted third parties. The following section provides details about the third-party cookies you may encounter while using our site.

Our website employs Google Analytics, one of the most widely recognized and trusted analytics solutions, to help us understand how you use our site and how we can enhance your experience. These cookies may track metrics such as your time spent on the site and the specific pages you visit, enabling us to produce engaging content.

For more information about Google Analytics cookies, please visit the official Google Analytics page.

More Information

We hope this explanation clarifies how we use cookies on our website. As previously mentioned, if you are uncertain about the necessity of cookies, it is generally safer to leave them enabled in case they interact with features you use on our site.
