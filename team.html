SeedConnect Africa Team

Chief Convener, SeedConnect Africa

Dr. Ishiak Khalid

Ag. Director General (NASC)

Dr. Ishiak Khalid is the Acting Director General of the National Agricultural Seeds Council (NASC). With a solid educational background and over three decades of experience, he has played a pivotal role in NASC's journey. Holding a BAgric. and MBA from the University of Ilorin, along with a PhD in Agricultural Economics from Tafawa Balewa University. Dr. Khalid's expertise and influence extends internationally, having participated in numerous agricultural committees, workshops and conferences. His leadership has been instrumental in promoting quality seed certification in Nigeria.

Convener, SeedConnect Africa

Folarin Okelola

Ag. Registrar, PVP Office

Dr. Folarin Sunday Okelola is a distinguished agricultural seed science and development expert, boasting over 16 years of extensive experience in seed industry coordination and management on a global scale. With a profound understanding of seed systems, agricultural innovations, enterprise development, private sector coordination, value chain management, research leadership, and agricultural seed policy formulation, Dr. Okelola is a recognized authority in the field.

Throughout his career, his unwavering commitment has been to enhance crop productivity through the application of cutting-edge seed technologies, foster seed industry growth, facilitate private sector engagement in the seed business, and champion the implementation of Plant Variety Protection (PVP) in Nigeria.

Co-Convener, SeedConnect Africa

Olasunkanmi Temitope

Head DUS, PVP Office

Mrs. Olasunkanmi Temitope is a highly accomplished professional in the field of plant breeding and seed technology, boasting an M.Sc. in Plant Breeding from Ahmadu Bello University and a B.Sc. in Plant Breeding and Seed Technology from the Federal University of Agriculture. Her career as a Senior Agricultural Officer at the National Agricultural Seeds Council in Abuja, Nigeria, since 2020, has been marked by significant contributions to agriculture and plant science. She has played a pivotal role in various endeavors, such as organizing SEEDCONNECT, demonstrating expertise in plant virology, establishing a GM Maize field to combat the African Fall Armyworm, and evaluating Soybean lines in collaboration with international organizations. She has also been instrumental in developing national crop descriptor fields and contributing to the drafting of the Plant Variety Protection (PVP) Law.

Furthermore, Mrs. Temitope has been dedicated to mentoring and supervising interns, facilitating seed policy meetings, conducting diverse laboratory analyses, and managing fields for collaborative projects with the Netherlands, all in the pursuit of advancing plant science and agriculture.

Co-Convener, SeedConnect Africa

Adelugba Kehinde

Head ICT, PVP Office

Kehinde Adelugba is a seasoned professional with a diverse background that combines expertise in digital agriculture, agricultural development, agricultural policy, and software product development. His experience in the agricultural sector has given him a deep understanding of the digital agriculture landscape, with a specific focus on the African context. He has also made significant contributions as an Independent Consultant, specializing in the development of innovative software solutions and tools for various institutions and organizations.
