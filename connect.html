SeedConnect Africa 2024

Seeds of Innovation, Harvests of Growth: Empowering Africa’s Agriculture Through Technology

We are excited to announce the 2024 SeedConnect Africa Conference and Exhibition, the premier event for stakeholders in the seed sector and the agricultural community across Africa. This year’s conference, with the theme “Leveraging Seed Technology to Scale Up Africa's Agricultural Productivity,” will be a hub for innovation, collaboration, and actionable insights aimed at advancing Africa’s agricultural productivity through the power of seed technology.

Africa stands at the crossroads of immense agricultural potential and the need for innovation. The 2024 SeedConnect Africa Conference will focus on how cutting-edge seed technologies, research, and policy initiatives can help increase crop yields, enhance food security, and drive sustainable growth across the continent. This event will bring together experts, innovators, farmers, agribusinesses, policymakers, and investors to discuss the latest advancements in seed technology and explore strategies to boost agricultural productivity in Africa.

Register today and secure your place at the 2024 SeedConnect Africa Conference and Exhibition. Together, we can unlock the potential of seed technology and drive Africa’s agricultural productivity to new heights!

Our Strategies

Strengthening Seed Research and Development

Proactively invest in high-yield, climate-resilient seeds tailored to Africa's diverse zones. Foster collaborations, harness experts' knowledge, and establish cutting-edge research facilities

Promoting Seed Access and Affordability

Develop policies for affordable, quality seeds, breaking cost barriers through last mile accessibility. Enable credit facilities, promote community seed banks, and ensure access for all small-scale farmers.

Seeds Education and Capacity Building

Launch farmer, certification officers, and policymaker training programs. Equip them with sustainable practices and seed technology expertise. Educate policymakers on seeds' pivotal role in food security.

Seed Advocacy and Policy Reforms

Advocate for growth-supporting policy reforms. Campaign for research-friendly regulations, lobby for industry investment, and shape favorable policies through dialogues with policymakers and stakeholders.

Seed Infrastructure Development & Investment

Promote investment in seed infrastructure — modern storage, processing centers, and efficient seed preparation & distribution. Safeguard seed quality, and improve logistics for timely, reliable distribution & delivery.

International Collaborations & Linkages

Engage in regional and global collaborations. Forge partnerships for cross-border seed expertise exchange, collaborate on research, and participate in knowledge-sharing initiatives.

Data-Driven Decision-Making

Consolidate on and establish more robust and advanced data systems for monitoring seed industry activities, crop yields, and food security. Leverage data analytics for informed resource allocation, strategic adjustments, and evidence-based strategies.
