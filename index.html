The SeedConnect is an annual event organized by the National Agricultural Seeds Council, NASC, Nigeria, in collaboration with the private seed sector to create a forum for major stakeholders within the industry to discuss various issues impacting players within the seed industry and design pathways for more resilient food systems.

SeedConnect is open to a wide range of participants, including industry leaders, researchers, entrepreneurs, investors, government officials, and farmers. Anyone interested in the seed industry, agricultural sustainability, and food security is welcome to attend.

The following are some of the great benefits you stand to gain by participating in the SeedConnect Africa 2024 Expo:
(i) Room for knowledge sharing and discussion to address key industry challenges
(ii) A platform and an opportunity for your company to exhibit your products and services to a global audience and also to access and view products and services from other businesses
(iii) Networking for actors both locally and internationally (iv) Opportunity to witness some of the latest innovations and products in the market

This year's conference will be holding at NAF Conference Centre, 496 Ahmadu Bello Way, Garki 2 900108, Abuja, Nigeria

For participants who would not be able to attend the event in person but would love to participate can attend the event virtually. A link to join to the event will be shared to your email once you register to attend and also will be shared across all our media platforms.

Yes, SeedConnect often provides opportunities for organizations to sponsor or exhibit at the event. If you're interested in becoming a sponsor or exhibitor, please contact the organizers for details on available packages and benefits.

Exhibiting at SeedConnect provides a unique opportunity to showcase your products, services, and innovations to a targeted audience within the seed industry. It allows you to engage with potential customers, partners, and collaborators directly.

To secure an exhibition booth, please contact the SeedConnect organizers via the contact information provided on the official website. They will guide you through the exhibition process, including booth options and pricing.

SeedConnect attracts a diverse range of exhibitors, including seed companies, agricultural technology providers, research institutions, and organizations offering services and solutions related to the seed industry.
